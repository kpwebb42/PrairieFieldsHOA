HOA goals:
  • Continue to maintain and protect the character and desirability of our neighborhood as commercial development continues in Savoy
  • Gauge interest among Prairie Field's residents about contracting with a single waste disposal/recycling company to provide service to the entire subdivision at a discounted rate and solicit bids
  • Work with residents to develop a wish list and schedule for capital improvement projects that may be completed with minimal or no increase to annual assessments.
